GraphAlgos will cover pass-by-pass configuration of these 5 algorithms
1. DFS: <SearchFor: Vertex>(May or may not be present in the graph), <Pred: Vertex[]>, <Visited[]: byte/state>
2. BFS: Same as DFS
3. Dijkstra Algorithm: <SearchFor: Vertex>(May or may not be present in the graph), <Pred: Vertex[]>, <Visited[]: byte/state>, <PathHeap: SortedSet<(Vertex:byte, Distance:int)>>
4. Floyd-Warshall Algorithm: <Vertex_Dist_Matrix: int[,]>
5. Kruskal's Algorithm: <EdgeList: (byte Weight, byte From, byte To)[]>, <Selected: bool[]>
